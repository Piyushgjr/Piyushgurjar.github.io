Master's Thesis

Bacterial degradation of Di-(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate (DEHP) and in silico analysis of interaction with Carboxylesterase Est2.

  • In this study, I employed bacterial strains and their consortium for degradation of DEHP a commonly used plasticizer
  • Further molecular analyses were done for best bacterial strain using computational techniques, then I modified the responsible enzyme and performed molecular docking to check their efficacy in wild type and engineered one
