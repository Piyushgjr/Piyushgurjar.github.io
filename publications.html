Featured Publications

Recent Development in Algae-Based Biostimulant Production for Sustainable Agriculture: Market Trend, Available Products, and Future Perspectives
Gurjar P., Kumar K., Kushwah S., Patel A.K., Karthikeyan O.P., Bhatia S.K., Zuorro A., Khare P., Mehariya S.
Bioresource Technology Reports (2026)
This review comprehensively discusses the recent advances in algae-based biostimulants for sustainable agriculture, covering cultivation strategies, extraction technologies, commercial products, market trends, regulatory frameworks and future perspectives. The article highlights the potential of microalgae to improve crop productivity while supporting environmentally sustainable agricultural practices.

Development of CNT Thin Film Electrodes for Lead Ion Sensing: A Step Toward Scalable Water Quality Monitoring
Gangwar A., Gurjar P., Gupta V.K., Dulay S., Varjos I., Gupta P.
Journal of Electroanalytical Chemistry (2026)
This work reports the fabrication and electrochemical evaluation of carbon nanotube thin-film electrodes for sensitive lead ion detection. The developed sensing platform demonstrates excellent analytical performance and offers potential for scalable water quality monitoring applications.
